About Us

Your Ideal Student Living Experience

At Haven Student Accommodation, we are dedicated to providing high-quality, affordable off-campus housing designed specifically for students. Our mission is to offer comfortable, secure, and conveniently located homes that enhance the university experience. Situated within walking distance of Nelson Mandela University, our residences are meticulously maintained to meet the highest standards of living.

We understand the unique needs of students and strive to create an environment that supports both academic success and personal well-being. Our commitment to excellence ensures that every student can enjoy a welcoming and functional living space, making Haven Student Accommodation the ideal choice for your home away from home.
